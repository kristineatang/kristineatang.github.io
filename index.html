WHO WE ARE

At Tine & Co., we're passionate about sustainability and reducing waste in the fashion industry. That's why we started selling recycled accessories back in 2020. We believe that fashion and sustainability can go hand-in-hand, and that's why we're dedicated to creating high-quality, eco-friendly accessories that not only look great but also do good for the planet. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful idea: to take discarded materials and turn them into unique, stylish pieces that not only reduce waste but also inspire a new wave of environmentally conscious consumers. With a focus on quality, style, and sustainability, we're committed to making a positive impact on the environment, one accessory at a time.

SUSTAINABLE STYLE

Offers stylish accessories made from recycled materials. Each piece is unique and helps reduce waste, allowing customers to express their personal style while making a positive impact.

UNIQUE TREASURES

One-of-a-kind finds, transformed from discarded items into beautiful, meaningful pieces. Customers can discover hidden gems and add individuality to their wardrobe while supporting a circular economy.
